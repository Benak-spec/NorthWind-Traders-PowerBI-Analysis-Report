**NorthWind Traders Analysis Report (Power BI)**



**Objective:**



I have used power bi to analyze NorthWind Traders a sales \& order data, including information on customers, products, orders, shippers, and employees. My report focuses mainly on business performance across customers, products, categories, orders.



**Tools Used:**

\- Power BI

\- Query Editor

\- DAX measures

\- CSV files



**Dashboard Pages:**

I divided the report into 4 dashboard pages

\- Executive Dashboard

\- Map

\- Product Category Detail

\- Customer Detail



**Key Insights:**

* Total revenue reached AED 1.4M
* 830 orders were recorded
* Beverages was the most ordered category
* Horst Kloss was the top customer by revenue
